Problem Statement

Urban Parking Is
Broken

Indian cities face a severe parking crisis. Drivers waste time, fuel, and patience circling for parking — while thousands of private spaces sit empty and underutilized.

01

No Centralized Parking Ecosystem

There is no unified platform connecting drivers to available parking infrastructure. Information is fragmented, outdated, and unreliable across Indian cities.

02

Fragmented Supply & Inefficiency

Private parking spaces — in malls, offices, residences — remain disconnected from demand. Existing supply is vastly underutilized while new infrastructure is costly to build.

03

User Frustration & Wasted Time

Drivers spend an average of 15–20 minutes searching for parking, contributing to congestion, pollution, and a degraded urban experience. The frustration is universal.

04

Growing Urban Density

With rapid urbanization, parking demand is outpacing supply. Indian cities need smart, scalable solutions — not more concrete structures.

Business Model

Revenue-Ready Marketplace

PARKIT operates as a two-sided marketplace connecting drivers with parking space owners — with multiple revenue streams designed for sustainable, scalable growth.

Dynamic Pricing Engine

AI-powered pricing that adjusts in real-time based on demand, location, time-of-day, and events. Maximizes revenue for space owners and optimizes cost for drivers.

Primary Revenue

Subscription Model

Monthly and annual plans for frequent parkers — guaranteed spots, priority access, and discounted rates. Recurring revenue stream with high retention potential.

Recurring Revenue

Marketplace Commission

PARKIT takes a commission on every booking made through the platform. The marketplace model requires no owned inventory — pure software-driven margins. Space owners list for free and pay only when they earn, creating a frictionless onboarding experience.
